About PhysicsLab

Our Mission

To transform secondary school physics education by making practical experiments and interactive learning accessible to every student and teacher across East Africa and beyond.

Educational Goals

  • Provide comprehensive theory aligned with national curricula.
  • Enable hands-on experimentation through guided practicals.
  • Foster creativity with student-led physics projects.
  • Use technology to track progress and personalize learning.

Platform Overview

PhysicsLab integrates modules, experiments, quizzes, and a dashboard into one seamless ecosystem. Built by Group 4, 2026.
